How to write a check for deposit

How to submit a check

  1. Write your name in the "Pay to the order of" section
  2. In the dollar section next to "Pay to the order of" section, write the amount for the check
  3. Write the date in the date section formatted in mm/dd/yyyy
  4. In the section below the "Pay to the order of" section, write out your check amount in words (i.e. Five-hundred)
  5. in the authorized Signature section, write your signature.
