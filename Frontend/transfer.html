Important Rules on Transfers

There are some important things you must keep in mind when running transfers in the real world.

Moving money around is very serious, and banks are required to follow all federal and state rules on how much can be moved at any given time.

Keep in mind that this page talks about transfers from you to another person. And not internal transactions

On the next page, you will see a list of the general laws behind bank transfers to keep in mind

Important Rules on Transfers (Continued)

  • Transactions of $10,000 or greater must be reported to the IRS

  • Most banks have limits on how much you can transfer in a day

  • 3rd party apps that run through your bank are supported by the provider and not your bank
